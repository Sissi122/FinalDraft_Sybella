About Us

Sina Gerard, a young and ambitious Rwandan, founded URWIBUTSO Enterprise in 1983 with a single employee. URWIBUTSO is a memorable phrase. It all began with a little shop in Ruhengeri or contemporary Musanze, and the bakery began operations in 1985 at Nyirangarama. Initially, SINA Gerard provided free beef stews and bread to tourists and truckers in order for them to sample his products. Later, in 1993, after ten years of business experience, SINA Gerard officially established his company under the name URWIBUTSO, which is delivered by individuals who have tried his beignets or amandazi.Today, the local food processing firm employs 421 people and has contracts with 3,000 farmers, making it one of Rwanda's largest businesses in its field. It is geared toward the agro-processing business. The firm is located in Nyirangarama, Rulindo District, Northern Province, approximately 45 kilometers from Kigali City, on the Kigali Musanze - Rubavu major route.


Mission: Processing and delivering standardized products and provide high quality services.

Vision: Being influential through providing excellent made in Rwanda products; Being competitive worldwide and Enhance social economic development.

Core values: God above all; Integrity within our commitment; Innovation; Bring to perfection our work; Reliability.


Ese URWIBUTSO / SINA GERARD Company which is ISO 9001:2000 and ISO 22000:2005 certified since 2007 is currently supporting more than 3000 local farmers to grow crops and fruits. For this, the farmers get seeds, technical advice and assistance as well as a secure market of their products from the company. It is also committed to strong community training, with clear practical training programs for farmers that include demonstration fields. Ese URWIBUTSO / SINA GERARD Company undertakes special projects which are critical to the economic and social development of local communities. That’s why it takes pride in the promotion and development of the community of Rulindo District and its surrounding. This is done by creating various rural socio-economic actions like the construction of roads leading to the farming areas. The construction and the equipment of schools for local children, where the education is free from nursery and primary we even have O’ level and A’level. Our students have international quality as our products are international and because they have all what it takes and all what they need, they perform well and win 100%. Those who study veterinary have livestock which helps them in doing their experiment and on which they practice surgery which helps them in knowing what the animals are sick of practically not only in theory. Those who study agronomy also have modern gardens which they use in their studies by cultivating them and controlling what they saw until the harvest and production. Ese URWIBUTSO / SINA GERARD company is committed to corporate social responsibility, promoting local development, distribution of cows to the local population with the system of mass redistribution of calves and heifers, creating job opportunities and training for farmers, environmental protection and workplace safety. All these activities have protected tourism in the area (ECOTourism).
